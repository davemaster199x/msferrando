  TDC is a 15-hour theoretical driving class and is one of the LTO’s requirements for Student Permit (SP) application. 

A TDC Certificate will be issued upon completion. 
Can be completed on the student’s availability. 
Schedules:
    Tue-Wed (8AM-5PM)
   Thur-Fri (8AM-5PM)
   Sat-Sun (8AM-5PM)

Requirements for SP: 
 TDC Certificate 
 PSA Certificate 
 Medical Certificate from an LTO Accredited Center  

PDC

Practical Driving Course

  PDC is a standard 8-hour driving course required by LTO to acquire a Non-Professional Driver’s License (NPDL) or when adding a restriction code for both Professional Driver’s License (PDL) and Non-Professional Driver’s License (NPDL).
  A student may need to exceed 8 hours if the LTO-Accredited instructor sees the student needs more time to master driving before certifying him or her.  

Requirements for NDPL: 
 PDC certificate (min of 8 hours)
 Medical certificate
 Police clearance
